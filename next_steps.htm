It doesn't matter who you are,
whether your family is dead, alive,
missing or has rejected you.

You now are a member of God's family
of believers and you have
brothers and sisters of faith.  

Be kind to yourself
as you begin your journey

You would not expect to learn to speak
a new language in a week,
so let the Holy Spirit that now
lives in you guide your actions,
thoughts and decisions day by day.

nor mother earth. 
 
God's love for you is personal.
